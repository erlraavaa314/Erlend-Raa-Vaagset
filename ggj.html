Global Game Jam: Førde

The Global Game Jam is a worldwide event where people come together to create games — digital or analog — in the span of a single weekend. It’s about creativity, collaboration, and playful experimentation.

I attended the first jam at HVL Førde (organized by Erlend S. and Gizem), and helped organize the second one in 2025. These events bring together students, developers, artists, and curious minds from across disciplines — and have become a unique space for creative energy and community at our campus.
