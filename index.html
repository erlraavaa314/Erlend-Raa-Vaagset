Hello!

You just discovered my own little corner of the web, congratulations! This is where I share information about myself, my work, teaching, as well as some of my favorite memes and web comics. Take a look around—you might find something surprising!

I completed my PhD at UiB in 2024 under the supervision of Nello Blaser and Mike Fellows. Before that, I got both a bachelor and master degree in mathematics supervised by Christian Schlichtkrull.

When I'm not teaching, I do research on impossible problems, try to uncover hidden connections, and do my best to communicate what I have learned. I invite you to explore these pages to find out more.
