Welcome!

I’m Erlend Raa Vågset, Assistant Professor in Computer Science at HVL. My background is in computational topology and parameterized complexity, where I’ve worked on NP-hard problems in topology. Recently, I’ve been expanding my focus to explore applications in robotics, data analysis, and AI.

I’ve also started venturing into pedagogy—both adopting new ideas in my own teaching but also as a research interest. I’m particularly interested in how our education system shapes independence, critical thinking, responsibility and belonging, especially in the context of democratic education and social learning in higher education.

Whether in research or teaching, I like working on difficult problems, uncovering hidden connections, and occasionally making sense of them. More than anything, I enjoy exploring new ways of thinking about the world— finding unexpected links between ideas and challenging assumptions. Take a look around—you might find something surprising!
