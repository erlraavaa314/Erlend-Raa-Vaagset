TL;DR

Hi, I’m Erlend Raa Vågset. I’m an Assistant Professor at HVL in Førde, Norway, where I focus on computational complexity, algorithms, and topology. My teaching focuses on making computer science fun and accessible, while my research explores how we can solve "impossible" problems—or at least, make them slightly less impossible.

At the crossroads of teaching and research, I aim to spark curiosity and uncover creative solutions to complex computational problems.
