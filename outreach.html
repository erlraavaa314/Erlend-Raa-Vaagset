Outreach

I am the founder and organizer of our local Kattis programming competitions, hosted in a social-learning format at the Læringslabben at HVL. The event runs alongside math drop-in sessions — with plenty of fresh waffles to go around.

These sessions are open to HVL students and local high schoolers, and are run in collaboration with Gizem and Camilla. We aim to make problem-solving and programming engaging, approachable, and fun.

I also volunteer at community tech events like the Global Game Jam, and I'm working on launching a SciSnack group at HVL Førde — a space for students and researchers to explore science writing, communication, and informal reflection together.
