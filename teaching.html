My Approach to Teaching

As an Assistant Professor at HVL, I aim to create a learning environment where students feel empowered to explore, collaborate, and grow. I believe in making learning hands-on, student-centered, and inclusive—fostering curiosity and confidence in every student.

Mentoring and Supervision

I see mentoring as a partnership. My role is to support and guide students as they tackle projects that inspire them—encouraging independence, creativity, and resilience along the way.

Whether it’s exploring machine learning applications or diving into theoretical computer science, I’m always excited to collaborate and help students bring their ideas to life.

Outreach and Community

I’m passionate about making computer science accessible to all. From school visits to coding workshops, I love creating opportunities for hands-on learning—whether it’s introducing children to sorting networks or exploring computational thinking with adults.
